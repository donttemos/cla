# CalcVerse 100-Post SEO Blog Content Map

Audience: English/global readers, with emphasis on founders, operators, growth teams, and practical utility-search users.

Goal: earn organic traffic with calculator-led educational content and send readers to relevant CalcVerse tools.

## Editorial Rules

- Use one primary keyword theme per article.
- Link each article to one primary calculator and no more than two secondary calculators.
- Use "estimate", "compare", "model", "plan", and "scenario" instead of absolute promises.
- Finance content must be educational and assumption-based, not financial, tax, or investment advice.
- Health content must be non-diagnostic and should recommend professional advice for personal medical concerns.
- AI Cost content must include an "updated as of" date during drafting and remind readers to verify official provider pricing.
- Conversion content must avoid universal benchmarks and separate correlation from causation.
- Suggested article structure: short answer, formula or calculation logic, example, common mistakes, calculator CTA, FAQ.
